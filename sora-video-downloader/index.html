How to Use Sora Video Downloader

Sora Video Downloader is designed to be simple and intuitive. Follow these steps to start downloading videos from Sora.com:

1
Install the Extension
Get Sora Video Downloader from the Chrome Web Store. Click "Add to Chrome" to install it in your browser.
2
Visit Sora.com
Navigate to Sora.com and browse the videos you're interested in. The extension will automatically activate on this site.
3
Locate the Download Button
Hover over any video to see the download button appear in the top-right corner of the video player.
4
Download the Video
Click the download button and your browser will automatically start downloading the video to your default download location.
5
Check Download Progress
A notification will appear showing the download progress and will notify you when the download is complete.

That's it! The video will be saved to your computer in MP4 format, ready for offline viewing.

Frequently Asked Questions

What video format will the downloads be in?
Videos are downloaded in their original format, typically MP4, which is compatible with most video players.
Does this extension work on websites other than Sora.com?
No, this extension is specifically designed to work only on Sora.com and will not function on other websites.
Is this an official Sora/OpenAI extension?
No, this is a third-party extension and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to OpenAI or Sora in any way.
Can I change where the videos are downloaded to?
The extension uses Chrome's default download system. You can change your default download location in Chrome's settings.
What permissions does the extension require?
The extension only requires minimal permissions: access to Sora.com, the ability to download files, and the ability to inject the download button into the page.
