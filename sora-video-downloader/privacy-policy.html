Introduction

Effective Date: April 5, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the "Sora Video Downloader" Chrome extension (referred to as "the extension", "we", "us", or "our") handles user data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Information Collection and Use

We do not collect any user data. The extension operates entirely on your local device and does not transmit any information to external servers or services.

What information we do NOT collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, etc.)
  • Browsing history
  • Videos you view or download
  • Websites you visit (other than Sora.com to enable functionality)
  • IP addresses or location data
  • Usage statistics

Temporary Data Processing

The extension temporarily processes video data only when you explicitly click the download button on a Sora video. This processing:

  1. Is performed entirely on your local device
  2. Is temporary (only held in memory while downloading)
  3. Is immediately discarded after the video file is saved
  4. Is never stored permanently or transmitted off your device except to save to your chosen location

Permissions

The extension requires certain permissions to function properly:

  • activeTab: To detect and interact with video elements on the current Sora.com tab
  • scripting: To inject the download button into the Sora video player
  • downloads: To save video files to your device
  • host_permissions (*.sora.com): To function only on the Sora.com website

These permissions are used solely for the purpose of downloading videos from Sora.com and nothing else.

Third-Party Services

The extension does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

The extension does not collect information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on the Chrome Web Store page for the extension. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@futuretoolsai.top.
