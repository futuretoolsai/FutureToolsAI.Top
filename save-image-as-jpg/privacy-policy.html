Privacy Policy

For the Save Image As JPG Chrome Extension

Effective Date: April 5, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the "Save Image As JPG" Chrome extension (referred to as "the extension", "we", "us", or "our") handles user data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Information Collection and Use

We do not collect any user data. The extension operates entirely on your local device and does not transmit any information to external servers or services.

What information we do NOT collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, etc.)
  • Browsing history
  • Images you view or convert
  • Websites you visit
  • IP addresses or location data
  • Usage statistics

Temporary Data Processing

The extension temporarily processes image data only when you explicitly right-click on an image and select "Save Image As JPG". This processing:

  1. Is performed entirely on your local device
  2. Is temporary (only held in memory while processing)
  3. Is immediately discarded after the JPG file is created
  4. Is never stored permanently or transmitted off your device

Permissions

The extension requires certain permissions to function properly:

  • contextMenus: To add the "Save Image As JPG" option to your right-click menu
  • downloads: To save the converted JPG file to your computer
  • scripting: To execute the code that converts the image format
  • activeTab: To access image content on the current webpage
  • host_permissions: To work on all websites

These permissions are used solely for the purpose of converting images to JPG format and nothing else.

Third-Party Services

The extension does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

The extension does not collect information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on the Chrome Web Store page for the extension. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@futuretoolsai.top.

Consent

By using our extension, you consent to our Privacy Policy.
